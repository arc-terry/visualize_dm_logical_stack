Conventional Commits references:
- https://www.conventionalcommits.org/en/v1.0.0/
- https://gist.github.com/qoomon/5dfcdf8eec66a051ecd85625518cfd13

Key points:
- Format: <type>[optional scope]: <description>
- Description is present tense, lowercase verb, no trailing period.
- Common types: feat, fix, docs, style, refactor, perf, test, chore, ci, build.
- Breaking changes: add ! after type/scope or use BREAKING CHANGE: footer.
